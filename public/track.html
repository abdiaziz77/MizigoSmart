Tracking FAQ

Common questions about shipment tracking

How often is tracking updated?

Tracking is updated in real-time. Our system receives updates every 15-30 minutes when the shipment is in transit, and immediately when there are status changes.

What do the different statuses mean?

Pending: Shipment booked but not yet picked up.
In Transit: Shipment is on the way to destination.
Out for Delivery: Final delivery in progress.
Delivered: Shipment successfully delivered.

Why is my tracking not updating?

If tracking isn't updating, it could be due to: network connectivity issues in transit areas, delays in scanning at facilities, or the shipment may be awaiting the next scheduled scan.

Can I change delivery instructions?

Yes, you can update delivery instructions up to 2 hours before the scheduled delivery time using the "Update Delivery" option in the tracking results.
